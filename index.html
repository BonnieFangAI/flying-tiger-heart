THE LEGACY

History That Lives On

In 1941, General Claire Lee Chennault led the American Volunteer Group — the legendary Flying Tigers — to fight alongside China against Japanese invasion. Their courage wrote one of WWII's most remarkable chapters. Now, their spirit takes flight again in the digital realm.

🛩️

The Flying Tigers

From 1941 to 1945, the American Volunteer Group, later the 14th Air Force, achieved legendary victories with their iconic shark-mouth P-40 Warhawks, scoring a 9:0 victory in their very first engagement.

⛰️

The Hump Route

Over 800km of death-defying flights across the Himalayas. 563 aircraft lost. 1,500+ lives sacrificed. 700,000 tons of supplies delivered over 3 years — a testament to unbreakable courage.

🤝

Friendship Endures

As President Xi Jinping said in 2023: "The Chinese people have not forgotten the Flying Tigers." This game honors that enduring bond between China and the United States.

GAMEPLAY

How It Works

Purchase blind boxes, collect fighter planes, form squadrons, and complete daily missions on a 1:1,000,000 scale map of mainland China to earn FTT tokens.

01

Purchase Blind Boxes

80 USDT per set of 3 fighter planes. Each purchase includes a 5U donation to Flying Tiger heritage institutions. 10-pull guarantees at least one Blue-tier or above plane.

02

Open & Discover

Unbox to discover fighter planes across 7 quality tiers — from Falcon to Legendary. Each box also yields fuel cards, ammunition cards, and armor cards to enhance your fleet.

03

Form Your Squadron

Assemble your fighter planes into combat squadrons. Accept rank promotions, enter the battle map, and choose daily missions suited to your fleet's capabilities.

04

Complete Missions & Earn

Dispatch planes on auto-combat missions. When the countdown ends, collect FTT rewards. Higher military ranks and corps levels multiply your earnings significantly.
